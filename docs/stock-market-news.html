Israeli Capital Market Highlights

  • The Tel Aviv Stock Exchange (TASE) has officially shifted its trading week to Monday through Friday, effective January 05, 2026. This change aligns the Israeli capital market with international standards, with Friday trading concluding at 1:50 PM or 2:00 PM local time.
  • This strategic move is intended to enhance the international appeal of the Israeli market, attract foreign investments, improve accessibility, increase liquidity, and facilitate the inclusion of Israeli stocks in major global indices.
