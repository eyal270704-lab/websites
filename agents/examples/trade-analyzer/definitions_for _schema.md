##### Setup Type Definitions - 

**perfect**: Golden Zone (0.5-0.618 Fib) + price above 150MA + no catalyst risk within 5 days

**momentum**: 20MA bounce or test + elevated RVOL (>1.2x) + strong relative strength

**breakout**: Clear support/resistance test + volume confirmation + pattern completion

**risky**: Valid technical setup exists BUT earnings or major catalyst within 5 days

**avoid**: No valid technical setup found - explain why in analysis field


##### Risk Score Guidelines -

**1-3 (Low Risk)**: Golden Zone setup, above 150MA, no earnings, volume confirming

**4-6 (Medium Risk)**: Valid setup but missing one criteria, or moderate catalyst exposure

**7-10 (High Risk)**: Earnings within 5 days, extended from MAs, low volume, or weak structure
